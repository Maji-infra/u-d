U-Shape Drain (T-25)

MAJI INFRA Precast Drop Lid U Shape is designed as per JIS (T-25). DU Series is a longitudinal drain and it is intended to be used at the shoulder of the road.

DUSD (T-25) is available in the following sizes.

Width: 300 ~ 1200 mm

Height: 300 ~ 1200 mm

Lids of DUSD (T-25) available in Standard Lid, No Hole Lid &amp; Perforated Lid options.
